Path of the Rabbit Samurai

Essentially a endless runner, where wolf ninjas come out of the trees on the side or run in from the front. Except there is an attack and block button to destroy the ninjas and block items thrown by enemies.

Murder Mystery

A game with an art style like last night, which is where the above image is from. It is more story centric and doesn't have too complex of mechanics.

Knight Duel

A 3D first person game where two players duel each other. The fight mechanics is using mouse to rotate sword, hold right mouse to lock rotation and move sword with mouse. Use left mouse button to attack, draw line to slash, click to thrust.

Life Runner

2D side scrolling runner where a person runs through life and make decisions that affects his or her survival. Choices are different paths, jump is only mechanic, each path has attribute, player dies when health hits 0.

School RPG

A top down classical rpg that satires high school life or college life. For example player fights tests in turnbased combat.

Incantation Showdown

A card game where players must chain together cards to summon monsters or spells. Each card is a symbol, can chain with two potential symbols, length of chain determines monster/spell.
